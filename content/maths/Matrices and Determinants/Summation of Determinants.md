# Summation of Determinants
Let $$\Delta(r)=\begin{vmatrix}
f(r) & g(r) & h(r) \\
a_1 & a_2 & a_3 \\
b_1 & b_2 & b_3 \\
\end{vmatrix}$$
where $a_1$, $b_1$... are independent of r. Then,
$$\sum_{r=1}^{n}\Delta(r)=
\begin{vmatrix}
\sum_{r=1}^{n}f(r)& \sum_{r=1}^{n}g(r) &  \sum_{r=1}^{n}h(r)\\
a_{1} & a_2 & a_3 \\
b_1 & b_2 & b_3 \\
\end{vmatrix}$$
